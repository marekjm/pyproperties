REMOVERS.

        pyproperties has to types of removers: remove and pop. 


    1)  remove()

            Is used for removing single properties from properties 
        dictionary.


    2)  removes()

            Used for removing groups of properties.
            Bases on the same re as gets() or sets().


    3)  pop()

            Removes single property and returns its value.


    4)  pops()

            Removes multiple properties form internal dict and returns 
        new dict - created from removed props.
